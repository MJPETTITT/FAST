RETURN ON EQUITY QUICK QUIZ

Let's check your understanding of ROE and the financial leverage effect with a quick quiz.

The use of debt…

1

Always leads to a higher return on equity.

2

Sometimes leads to a higher return on equity.

3

Never leads to a higher return on equity.

Not Quite

The use of debt leads to a higher return on equity if its return on assets exceeds its cost of debt.

RETURN ON EQUITY QUICK QUIZ

Cash Converter earns a 14 percent return on equity. Which of the following will cause the return on equity to increase?

1

Issuing a 9% bond and investing the proceeds to earn 12%.

2

Increasing the dividend.

3

An increase in the market share price on the stock market.

Not Quite

Investing at 12% with debt that costs 9% creates a positive financial leverage effect, as the return exceeds the cost of debt.

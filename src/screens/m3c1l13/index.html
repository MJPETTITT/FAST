Understanding EBIT

What would Aquasail's EBIT be under this assumption?

Assume the estimated lifetime of the machinery would be 8 years. You previously calculated quarterly depreciation for the machinery over 8 years to be 150. Remember, you can review all operating costs on Aquasail's profit-and-loss statement by opening your workbook.

1

1750

Answer

The EBIT equals 1750, as depreciation would be 150 lower compared to the previous case, hence expenses would be lower and EBIT higher. Note that, in this situation, Aquasail would report a higher earning, but its cash situation would not change.

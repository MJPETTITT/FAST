[YEAR 1 QUARTER 2] PROFIT-AND-LOSS STATEMENT [1]

Selling 3 boats earns Aquasail 6600. Wages, services and depreciation are the same as in the first quarter.

Operating revenues: earned from the sale of 3 boats = 6600

Production costs:

  • Wages: 2000
  • Services: 800

But what is the cost related to the materials? Remember that expenses relate to the use of assets.

Inventory of raw materials is an asset of Aquasail. While it has bought 6000 materials, it has only used 4000 to assemble its 4 sailboats. The remaining 2000 is an asset that Aquasail still possesses, and can use next quarter. The cost related to the use of raw materials is hence 4000, not 6000. Note that the cost of production, relating to the 4 assembled boats, did not change. It is again 7200.

PROFITABILITY

We now will analyse Aquasail’s profitability. This is important for shareholders, as they aim to get a decent return on their investment, that should compensate them for the risk they took by investing in the startup. But first, we take an operational perspective. Was Aquasail able to generate a sufficient return on all the assets it employed in its boating business?

In this module you will:

  • Calculate Aquasail’s operational profitability, including its (net) margin and asset turnover.

  • Calculate Aquasail’s return on equity.

  • Estimate Aquasail’s financial leverage effect.

  • Interpret Aquasail’s first year profitability.

At the end of this module you will be able to:

  • Define a required level of profitability.

  • Analyse how the return on equity is driven by a firm’s margin, asset turnover and financial leverage effect.

  • Interpret the profitability of a firm.

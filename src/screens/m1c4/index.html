The Journey Ahead

All managerial decisions, in whatever department, affect an organisation’s financial health.

Select each item to learn more.

In this primer, you will

  • Start a fictitious sailboat company, Aquasail, and run it as manager for 2 years.

  • Raise cash from different sources for investments and for running the daily operations.

  • Develop quarterly cash flow and income statements, based upon Aquasail’s activities.

  • Develop annual balance sheets.

  • Analyse the annual financial statements to understand Aquasail’s short term and long term financial risk, and its profitability.

  • Suggest actions to improve Aquasail’s overall financial health.

By the end of this primer, you will

  • Have a broad understanding of an organisation’s financial statements: balance sheet, income statement and cash flow statement and how they fit together.

  • Understand how operational decisions impact an organisation’s financial statements.

  • Be able to analyse an organisation’s short term and long term financial risk, based upon its financial statements.

  • Be able to analyse a firm’s profitability and understand its different components.

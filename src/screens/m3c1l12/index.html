Understanding Depreciation

Over 4 years, the quarterly depreciation of the machinery equals 4800 total value divided by 16 quarters = 300. Assume the estimated lifetime of the machinery would be 8 years, rather than 4 years. Calculate the answer to this quick question (you may use a calculator!):

What would the quarterly depreciation related to the machinery be?

Answer

Quarterly depreciation of the machinery would be 150 = 4800/32. A longer lifetime leads to lower depreciation.

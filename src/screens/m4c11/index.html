Which statement is wrong?

1

In a financially healthy company, the working capital requirement is approximately equal to the available net working capital.

2

Retailers have usually a negative working capital requirement.

3

In order to reduce the liquidity risk, it is optimal to finance the working capital requirement with either equity or long term liabilities.

4

The working capital requirement of an organisation will expand if the days inventory outstanding decreases (everything else being equal).

Not Quite

By decreasing the days inventory outstanding, the working capital requirement will decrease.

1

Low

Liquidity Quick Quiz

Which of the following minimises a firm’s working capital requirement? (Select all that apply).

Switching to on-line sales, rather than selling through distributors.

Switching to exports to Southern European countries, where payment terms are typically longer.

Increasing sales in a company with a positive WCR, while keeping DSO, DIO and DPO constant.

Negotiating longer payment terms from suppliers.

Not Quite

Switching to on-line sales decreases DSO and hence WCR, as customers pay immediately when buying on-line, while distributors pay after they have sold. But longer payment terms from suppliers also lead to lower WCR, thanks to higher DPO.

MINI-LECTURE: PROFIT-AND-LOSS STATEMENT

Was Aquasail profitable?

Aquasail’s cash increased from 21200 to 23200, but was Aquasail profitable as well? A profit-and-loss statement records all revenues and expenses incurred during a period; the difference between both is the profit or loss incurred during the period. It is hence very comparable to a cash flow statement, but not exactly the same.

There is some overlap between both, as some (but not all) cash payments are expenses and some (but not all) cash receipts are revenues: they are not identical.

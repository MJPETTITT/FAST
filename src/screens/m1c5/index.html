HOW TO USE THIS PRIMER

You will need to spend at least 4 to 5 hours working through this primer, ideally in 1 session. There are 2 main components to this primer: the Aquasail simulation and the FAST workbook.

As you will be interacting with a simulation in this primer, we recommend that you use a larger size screen or monitor to view it, and expand the browser window to full size.

Use the arrows on the left side of the screen to learn more.

PLAYING AN ACTIVE PART

You must pay close attention and think carefully about what you are being taught and what you are being asked to do, as by the end of this primer you need to be able to understand and fill in a cash flow statement and a profit-and-loss statement (P&L, also known as an income statement), calculate ratios, and perform other basic accounting tasks.

So don't expect to just sit back and be fed information - you are expected to play a very active part in your learning! We strongly recommend that you have a notepad beside you and take notes throughout, to help you remember the concepts and ratios you will encounter.

THE AQUASAIL SIMULATION

The Aquasail simulation enables you to physically move cash and resources through the Aquasail business, and to track cash inflows and outflows over the first 2 years of its operation. On the next screen you'll work through a short tutorial before you work with the simulation itself, to familiarise you with the basic functionality and layout. You will visit the Aquasail simulation on numerous pages. Each time you will move cash and/or resources.

THE FAST WORKBOOK

Every time you move cash in the Aquasail business you will record this transaction in the on-screen FAST workbook. This workbook contains the basic financial and accounting documents needed to run the business. You may also want to download a blank version of the workbook (available in the menu and the workbook overlay) and print it out so you can see all the financial documents together.

Physically moving cash and resources in the Aquasail simulation will enable you to see visually how a business works. Updating the FAST workbook each time will enable you to understand how each transaction is recorded in the accounting documents. So by the end of this primer you will have a strong understanding of the relationship between the financial transactions that occur within a business and how these are recorded.

LECTURES

Along the way you will also receive a number of mini-lectures on key finance and accounting concepts, as well as in-depth lectures on how to analyse the data you have recorded in your FAST workbook.

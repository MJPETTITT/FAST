SOLVENCY

A third pillar of the financial health of a company is its long term financial risk. It is not because a company has a good short term liquidity position, or has a satisfactory profitability, that it is well positioned to survive in the long term. Its strategy will be the main driver of its long term success. But its financials may hamper long term growth, or sustain it. This will depend on how much debt the company raised in the past, and, more specifically, whether its operations generate sufficient cash to meet its financial obligations. This is what a solvency analysis will find out.

In this module, you will:

  • Calculate Aquasail’s static solvency ratios.

  • Calculate Aquasail’s dynamic solvency ratios, including its debt repayment capacity.

  • Interpret Aquasail’s solvency or long term financial risk.

At the end of this module you will be able to:

  • Calculate the static solvency ratios.

  • Analyse a firm’s long-term debt repayment capacity.

  • Analyse the solvency risk (or long term financial risk) of a firm.

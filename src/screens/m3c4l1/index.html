[YEAR 1 QUARTER 4] INTRODUCTION

We are now in the fourth quarter. In this quarter you will:

  • Assemble 4 sailboats and sell 2.
  • Pay employees and pay the outstanding invoice to suppliers.
  • Record each movement in the cash flow statement.
  • Calculate the quarter 4 EBIT.
  • Pay the bank.
  • Pay taxes.
  • Develop the end-of-year balance sheet.

At the end of this quarter you will be able to:

  • Understand how interest payments on bank debt, and repayment of the principal amount, impact cash and profits.
  • Develop a full-fledged balance sheet.
  • Understand what a company can do with its earnings.
  • Understand that equity is comprised of the initial capital raised and retained earnings.

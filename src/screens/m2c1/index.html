The Startup Phase

It is time to start your company. In this startup phase you will:

  • Raise money from shareholders and the bank, and simulate how this money comes into the company.

  • Invest in land, buildings and equipment, and simulate the payment thereof.

  • Develop first cash flow statements and balance sheets reflecting these financing and investment actions.

At the end of this startup phase you will be able to:

  • Understand that a cash flow statement reflects all cash receipts and payments a company makes during a period.

  • Understand that a balance sheet is a picture of all sources and uses of funds.

  • Understand that assets should always equal equity and liabilities.

  • Understand the difference between equity and debt.

  • Understand how the use of debt increases a firm’s financial risk.

Mini-Lecture: Balance Sheet

Now we will look at the balance sheet. The balance sheet is a picture of Aquasail’s sources of funds, which you find on the right-hand side of the document, and its use of funds, which you find on the left-hand side. The balance sheet totals at the right-hand side and the left-hand side should be equal at all points in time.

[YEAR 1 QUARTER 2] INTRODUCTION

In Quarter 2, Aquasail will assemble 4 sailboats, but now Aquasail will build up some inventory. How will that affect cash and EBIT?

In this quarter you will:

  • Assemble 4 sailboats and and sell 2.
  • Pay suppliers and employees, and collect money from customers.
  • Record each movement in the cash flow and income statement.

At the end of this quarter you will be able to:

  • Understand how inventory changes impact cash and EBIT.
  • Understand how different cost accounting methods, such as direct cost or full cost, impact EBIT when inventories change.

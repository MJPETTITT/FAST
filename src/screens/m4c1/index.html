LIQUIDITY

Once a comprehensive set of financial statements are produced, including the balance sheet, income statement and cash flow statement, these statements are useful to understand and analyse the financial health of a firm.

In this module you will:

  • Calculate Aquasail’s static liquidity ratios, including current ratio and acid test.
  • Calculate Aquasail’s dynamic liquidity ratios, including net working capital and working capital requirement.
  • Interpret Aquasail’s liquidity risk.

At the end of this module you will be able to:

  • Understand the static liquidity ratios, current ratio and acid test.
  • Understand why these ratios give a limited view of a firm’s short term financial risk.
  • Understand the dynamic concepts of net working capital and working capital requirement.
  • Analyse the liquidity risk (or short term financial risk) of a firm.

[YEAR 1 QUARTER 1] INTRODUCTION

You will now run Aquasail, working in quarters. In this quarter you will:

  • Buy raw materials and equipment.
  • Assemble and sell sailboats.
  • Simulate all payments, and keep track of the payments in the cash flow statement.
  • Estimate Aquasail’s operating profit or EBIT, realised in the first quarter.

At the end of this quarter you will be able to:

  • Understand how operational actions translate in financial statements, especially cash flow and income statements.
  • Understand the concept of depreciation, and its impact on cash and earnings.
  • Estimate the earnings before interest and taxes.
  • Understand the difference between cash and earnings.

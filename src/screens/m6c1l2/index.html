YEAR 2 BUDGET

Select the workbook overlay to view the Year 2 budget.

Welcome to Aquasail’s second year. First, make sure that you understand Aquasail’s new budget for Year 2. The main change with Year 1 is that Aquasail will only assemble 2 boats per quarter, rather than 4, in order to decrease its inventory of boats.

The direct costs to assemble a boat do not change: wages amount to 500 and raw materials to 1000. But quarterly services drop to 600. Depreciations do not change either, at 400 per quarter.

As Aquasail will now assemble 2 boats per quarter, you only buy 2 units of raw materials per quarter, amounting to 2000. Note that Aquasail always keeps an inventory of 2000 of raw materials. Raw materials are always paid with 1 quarter payment delay.

The sales price remains 2200 per boat. Aquasail will sell 3 boats in the first quarter. No sales are paid cash; you always send an invoice to the customers which you collect the next quarter. In Year 2, Aquasail will sell again sell 11 boats, the same number as in Year 1.

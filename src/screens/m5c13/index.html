Did Aquasail create value?

Remember that Aquasail’s operations generated a return on its assets (ROA) of 7,38%, which resulted in a return on equity after taxes (ROE) of 2,1%. While we do not know which risk premium Aquasail’s shareholders expect, we know that the bank asks a 10% interest on its debt. It’s clear that Aquasail was not able to generate a return that was sufficient to compensate its bank, let alone its shareholders.
